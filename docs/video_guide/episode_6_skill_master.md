# Episode 6: The Skill Master Villager

**Scene**: A village with a custom-built Skill Master house. A Skill Master is standing behind a Skill Scribe Table.

---

## 🎙️ Script

**[Intro]**
"We've covered a lot of technical ground, but how does this feel for the player? Today, we're meeting the **Skill Master**. This isn't just another villager specialized for trades—it’s a dynamic merchant that grows with your character."

**[The Workstation]**
"To get a Skill Master, you need a **Skill Scribe Table**. Place it down, and a villager will take the profession. They have 5 tiers, just like standard villagers, but their trades are driven by your reputation and progression."

**[Dynamic Trades]**
"Initially, they'll offer basic Skill Tomes and Sigils. But as you trade and gain reputation, they unlock higher tiers. At Tier 5, they can offer Max Level Skill Tomes and rare artifacts like the Tome of Greater Clear Mind. 
The prices aren't static—they scale based on your current level in the skill they're selling. If you're already level 5, a tome to get to level 6 might be cheaper because you've already proven your mastery."

**[Configuration]**
"As a pack creator, you have total control. In your datapack under `data/puffish_skill_leveling/skill_master_reputation/config.json`, you can tweak the base experience per trade, set price multipliers, and even define custom trades for specific villager tiers. You can make certain skills only obtainable through the villager, turning them into a true keeper of knowledge."

**[Loot & Houses]**
"The mod also includes custom structure loot. If you find a Skill Master's house in the wild, check the barrels! You'll find Sigils, Scrap Tomes, and maybe even a rare Tome of Progression. It adds a layer of discovery to your world's exploration."

**[Outro]**
"The Skill Master is your player's best friend. Next time, we're talking about **Loot Modes**—how to decide which skills come from the tree and which come from the world."
