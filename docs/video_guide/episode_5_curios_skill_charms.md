# Episode 5: Curios Integration & Skill Charms

**Scene**: Player opening the Curios inventory menu, showing a Skill Charm in a dedicated slot.

---

## 🎙️ Script

**[Intro]**
"Armor and weapons are only half the story. Today we're exploring **Curios Integration**. If you have the Curios API installed, this addon unlocks a powerful new way to customize your character: **Skill Charms**."

**[What are Skill Charms?]**
"Skill Charms are a dedicated item type that fits into your Curio slots. By default, they come with internal Equipment Slots just like your gear. You can use Sigils to expand them and then imbue them with whatever tomes you find in your adventures."

**[Instant Activation]**
"The big difference with Skill Charms is **Instant Activation**. While gear might require you to be a certain level in the skill tree, Skill Charms are designed to grant their bonuses immediately upon being equipped. This makes them perfect for rare artifacts or specialized builds that don't want to invest hundreds of points into a specific tree."

**[Configuring Slots]**
"As a creator, you can control these slots via datapacks. Looking at the `curios/slots/skill_charm.json` file, you can define how many charm slots a player starts with. You can even tie these slots to specific entities or progression milestones."

**[Gating Curios]**
"If you feel Skill Charms are too powerful, we've added a config option: `requireUnlockForCurioImbuing`. If this is true, players will still need to unlock the base skill in their tree before the charm's bonuses will activate. It's the ultimate tool for balancing your endgame."

**[Visual Check]**
"Check out my movement speed. I swap my 'Swiftness' charm for a 'Strength' charm, and you can see my FOV and damage stats update the millisecond the item hits the slot. It’s smooth, seamless, and adds a whole new layer to your loadouts."

**[Outro]**
"Now your players are geared up and charmed out. But where do they get these tomes and sigils? In the next episode, we’re meeting the **Skill Master villager**!"
