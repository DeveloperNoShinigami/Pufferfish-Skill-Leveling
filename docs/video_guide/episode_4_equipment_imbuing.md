# Episode 4: The Equipment Imbuing System

**Scene**: Anvil interface with a Diamond Sword and a Skill Tome.

---

## 🎙️ Script

**[Intro]**
"Welcome back. Today we're looking at one of the coolest features of this addon: **Equipment Imbuing**. This lets your players take the skills you've defined and bake them directly into their armor, weapons, and tools."

**[Equipment Slots]**
"Before you can imbue an item, it needs an **Equipment Slot**. Every piece of gear starts with zero, but by using a **Sigil of Imbuement** in an anvil, you can open up to 3 slots. 
You'll see a new line on the item tooltip: '◈ Equipment Slots: 0/1'. This is the 'Equipment' branding we use throughout the mod to distinguish it from the standard skill tree."

**[Imbuing with Skill Tomes]**
"Once you have a slot, you need a **Skill Tome**. These items are configured to hold a specific skill at a specific level. Put your sword in the first anvil slot and the tome in the second. 
The result? A weapon that grants you those skill bonuses whenever you hold or wear it. You'll see a clean **✦ (Star)** symbol next to the skill name on the gear."

**[Upgrading & Cleansing]**
"You can upgrade imbued skills by adding more tomes of the same type. And if you want that skill back? Use a **Tome of Cleansing**. There are three tiers—Cleansing I, II, and III—targeting the first, second, or third slot respectively. Extracting the skill returns it as a tome, leaving the slot open for something new."

**[Dynamic Updates]**
"Notice my health bar. As soon as I wear this chestplate imbued with 'Vitality', my extra hearts appear. As soon as I take it off, they're gone. It's fast, efficient, and perfect for gear-based progression."

**[Outro]**
"Gear is great, but what about the things you wear *between* the armor? Next time, we're looking at **Curios Integration** and the new **Skill Charms**!"
